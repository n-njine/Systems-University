## HOW THE AI SUPERCHARGES YOUR LEARNING

Throughout this curriculum, an AI-powered companion (imagine a custom GPT‑4 + code‑execution environment) works alongside you:

1. **Adaptive Problem Selection** – Based on your recent submissions, the AI builds a daily practice set that targets your current edge of competence (the “sweet spot” for growth). It ensures you don’t waste time on already‑mastered topics and don’t get crushed by too‑hard material.

2. **Instant Personal Tutor** – When you’re stuck, the AI gives hints, not whole solutions. It can explain algorithms in multiple modalities — text, step‑by‑step pseudo‑code, interactive visualisations, and even convert mathematical proofs into intuitive sketches.

3. **Automated Code Review** – Every submission is analysed for common inefficiencies (e.g., `map` instead of `unordered_map`, missing `reserve`, over‑copying). It teaches you to write “contest‑grade” C++ that never gets TLE from a constant factor.

4. **Weakness Heatmaps & Spaced Repetition** – The AI tracks your error patterns across months and schedules revision of fragile topics just before you’d forget them. If you constantly fail on “Dijkstra + state”, it will inject a re‑learning module with fresh problems.

5. **Simulated Team Environment** – During ICPC prep, the AI simulates a “third teammate” if you’re training solo, or analyses team log data to suggest better division of labour (e.g., “Alice should never touch string problems in the last hour — assign them to Bob”).

6. **Library Generation** – As you master a new data structure, the AI helps you craft a bug‑free, generic snippet and stores it in a searchable, auto‑tagged code library. Before a contest, it regenerates the most likely needed templates based on the problem forecast.
